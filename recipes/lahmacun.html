Lahmacun

What is Lahmacun

Lahmacun is a kind of stuffed pita in Middle Eastern cuisine made by spreading a mixture of minced meat, parsley, onion, onion, garlic, black pepper and spices such as isot (red pepper) on the rolled dough and baking it in a stone oven.

Ingredients

  • minced meat
  • onion
  • parsley
  • green peppers
  • tomato paste
  • garlic
  • tomato
  • flour

Steps

  1. For the dough, sift 4 cups of flour into a bowl. Add dry yeast, olive oil and salt.
  2. Add warm water into the mixture little by little and knead the dough until it reaches a consistency that will not stick to your hands.
  3. Cover the dough with a damp cloth and let it rest.
  4. For the filling, pass all the ingredients except the tail fat and minced meat through a food processor and add the spices.
  5. Add finely ground tail fat and minced meat into the mixture and knead it.
  6. Divide the rested dough into equal pieces and roll out the pieces with a rolling pin.
  7. Add the minced meat mixture on the rolled out dough and spread it all over the surface.
  8. Place the lahmacuns on the baking tray lined with greaseproof paper. Bake the lahmacuns in a preheated oven at 180 degrees until golden brown. Enjoy your meal already.
