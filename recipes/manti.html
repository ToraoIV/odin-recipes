Manti

What is Manti

Manti is a dish made by putting minced meat seasoned with various spices into small pieces of dough and boiling these pieces of dough in water.

Ingredients

  • flour
  • minced meat
  • parsley
  • tomato paste
  • yogurt
  • egg
  • garlic
  • onion

Steps

  1. First, whisk the water, salt and egg in a bowl. When the whisking process is complete, add flour and start kneading.
  2. If the dough is too soft, you can add flour a little at a time. Cover and let it rest for 30 minutes.
  3. Grate the onion you will use for the mixture and add it to the minced meat. Add salt, red pepper flakes and black pepper to the minced meat. Different spices can also be added upon request.
  4. Roll out the dough on a floured bench and cut small squares. Fill them with minced meat mixture and close them in the shape of a bundle.
  5. Do the same for all the manti.
  6. Meanwhile, add water and salt into the pot and start boiling. Cook the prepared ravioli in boiling water.
  7. For the topping, melt the butter and fry the tomato paste.
  8. Transfer to the manti pot and cook for 10 more minutes. Place on the service plate.
  9. You can add garlic yogurt, mint and sumac on top.
