Sarma

What is Sarma

It is a dish made by putting bulgur or rice and other filling ingredients depending on the region into the vine leaves.

Ingredients

  • vine leaves
  • olive oil
  • onion
  • tomato
  • rice

Steps

  1. Firstly, soak the pickled leaves in hot water for 2-3 minutes, wash them and put them in a colander. Grate the onions into a large bowl.
  2. Pour olive oil on it.
  3. Wash the rice and add it into the bowl.
  4. After adding the spices, tomato paste and salt, add finely chopped parsley and mix. (it will be a raw filling)
  5. Put the filling on the wide part of the leaf and wrap it like a roll by closing it from both sides. Continue this process until the leaves are finished.
  6. Add olive oil and lemon slices on the wrapped leaves, add about 5-6 cups of hot water and cook over low heat.
